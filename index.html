The Mission

Our democracy is broken — not because Americans disagree, but because the system rewards division over solutions. Oath & Action is about fixing the machine, not picking a side.

Every issue explores the structural reforms — ranked choice voting, open primaries, independent redistricting — that can return power to voters and break the stranglehold of partisan extremes. No party. Just country.

About the Author

Dustin Cathcart

I led soldiers in Iraq, earned a Bronze Star, then got an MBA from Dartmouth and an MPA from Harvard. I built a business, raised two daughters with my wife in Massachusetts — and somewhere along the way realized the system we all swore to defend wasn't defending us back.

Now I'm an entrepreneur turned democracy advocate, serving as Massachusetts State Lead for Veterans for All Voters. I write this newsletter because I want my girls to grow up in a country where your vote actually matters.

No party. Just country.
